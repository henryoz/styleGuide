Buttons

Below you will find all the different types of buttons available to use. Alongside each type you will find a guide for their suggested use and the markup required.

Default Buttons

When to use them:

  • These buttons are for general purpose use and should be the most frequently used style of button on the site.
  • Upper or lowercase? To be discussed.

<button>Click me</button>

<a href="#" class="button">No, click me!</a>

No, click me!

Secondary Buttons

When to use them:

  • There may or not be a use for a second style of button.
  • Upper or lowercase? To be discussed.

<button class="secondary">Click me</button>

<a href="#" class="button secondary">No, click me!</a>

No, click me!
